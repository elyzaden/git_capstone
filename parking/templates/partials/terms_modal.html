  2. Reservation Policy
    1. Parking Period and Payment: Users are required to specify the start date and time for their desired parking period in the reservation form. After making their selection, a summary of the reservation details is displayed, and users are then directed to the payment form to settle the parking reservation fee. If the user completes the payment immediately, the reservation becomes binding, and the chosen slot is reserved for the user by PocketPark. In cases where the user prefers to pay at a later time, a 5-minute period is provided to finalize payment and secure the chosen parking spot. If payment is not made within the allocated time frame, the chosen slot becomes subject to availability, and the reservation is considered void.
    2. Reservation Confirmation: Upon completing the reservation or booking process, PocketPark will promptly confirm it by sending an SMS message to the contact number provided by the user. This confirmation serves as official proof of the reservation made through PocketPark.
    3. Entry and Exit Conditions: Entering the parking facility at a date/time earlier or later than the parking period start date or exiting the parking facility at a date/time earlier or later than the parking period end date is possible, subject to the following conditions:
      1. If a user enters the parking facility before the scheduled start date and time of their reservation, they must wait until their specified reservation time begins before utilizing the reserved parking spot for a fair and orderly process.
      2. If a user enters the parking facility after the reservation's start date and time, it does not affect the end date and time of the reservation; the end remains unchanged. No refunds for any portion of the parking fees are provided.
      3. When a user exits the parking facility with their vehicle before the end date and time of the reservation, the reservation ends when the user leaves the facility. Re-entry during the reservation period is not permitted; the reservation ends automatically upon the first exit, and no refunds are issued.
      4. If a user exits the parking facility after the end date and time of the reservation, they will be charged for the additional time. The user must pay the full daily rate for the extended duration, and this charge should be settled in person with the parking staff.
    4. Parking Spot Reservation Confirmation: To avoid any instances where a user parks in the spot reserved by another parking renter and to prevent confusion or conflicts, the establishment employs a clear reservation confirmation process. In cases where a user insists that a parking spot is rightfully theirs, they must adhere to the following protocol:

    5. a. Reserved Signs are placed in reserved parking spots to clearly indicate their status. These serve as visual markers to help prevent parking in the wrong spot.

    6. b. If a user believes that a parking spot is reserved for them, they must present their reservation details, including the reservation receipt and QR Code, to the parking staff or parking facility personnel.

    7. c. The parking staff will verify the user's reservation details and the spot's availability. If the reservation details and QR Code match the spot in question, the user's statement will be confirmed.

  5. Parking Scope
    1. Vehicle Type: The Pocketpark system is exclusively designed for reserving parking spaces for standard-sized cars (sedans, SUVs, pickup trucks, hatchbacks, and similar vehicles). Users are not permitted to book spaces for other vehicle types such as trucks, motorcycles, or larger vehicles like RVs or buses.
  6. Grace Period and Extension Policy
    1. Initial Grace Period: The system offers a 15-minute free grace period based on the indicated reservation start time. During this grace period, users have the flexibility to arrive at the parking facility without incurring any additional fees. The grace period is designed to accommodate unforeseen delays and ensure a convenient parking experience.
    2. Grace Period Extension: Users who require more time beyond the initial 15-minute grace period have the option to extend it. The extension allows users to extend the grace period, depending on their needs. Users are not obligated to extend the grace period and may choose to enter the parking facility at any time within the reservation time, initial or extended grace period.
    3. Limit on Extension: For a single reservation, users are allowed to extend the grace period only once. After this single allowable extension, if the user does not arrive within the extended grace period, the reservation will become invalid. Users may proceed to regular or traditional parking or they have the option to create a new reservation once the previous one has expired or become invalid.
