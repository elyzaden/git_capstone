MAYFLOWER PARKING GUIDELINES

1. Please adhere to designated parking zones as indicated by signage and markings.

2. Maintain a safe and cautious speed of 5 mph within the parking premises.

3. Respect reserved parking spaces for individuals with specific permits or authorization.

4. Ensure payment for parking is made promptly using designated payment methods.

5. Lock your vehicle and avoid leaving valuables in plain sight. The management is not responsible for any theft or damage.

6. Dispose of trash and litter in designated receptacles.

7. In case of emergencies or concerns, contact the establishment's management or security personnel.

8. Smoking is prohibited within the parking facility.

9. Present the provided QR code for entry at the establishment's entrance or guard booth.

Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in towing or other necessary actions. We appreciate your cooperation and hope you have a pleasant parking experience.
